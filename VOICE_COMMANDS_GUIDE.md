# 🎙️ Trading Engineer: Voice Command Documentation

Yeh guide aapko batayegi ki kaise aap poore trading dashboard ko sirf apni awaaz (voice) se control kar sakte hain. Humne ismein **English** aur **Hinglish** dono ka support diya hai.

---

## 🚀 Navigation Commands (Page Switchers)
Dashboard ke alag-alag sections mein jane ke liye niche diye gaye commands bole:

| Command (English) | Command (Hinglish) | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "Go to Overview" | "Dashboard dikhao" | Home page/Overview par le jayega |
| "Go to Stocks" | "Sare stocks dikhao" | All Stocks table/cards open karega |
| "Show Momentum" | "Momentum radar kholo" | **New** Momentum & Breakout Radar dikhayega |
| "Go to AI Research" | "AI research pe chalo" | Smart Rankings aur Analysis dikhayega |
| "Show News" | "Market news dikhao" | News Intelligence feed open karega |
| "Go to Watchlist" | "Meri watchlist dikhao" | Aapke favorite stocks ki list dikhayega |
| "Show Alerts" | "Alerts check karo" | Live price aur signal alerts dikhayega |

---

## 🔍 Search & Analysis (Stock Specific)
Kisi khas stock ke baare mein janne ke liye:

- **Specific Stock Analyze**: 
    - *"Analyze Reliance"* ya *"TCS ka analysis dikhao"*
- **Search Open**: 
    - *"Open Search"*, *"Find Stock"*, ya *"Khojo"* (Isse Search bar open ho jayega)
- **Top Stocks**: 
    - *"Show best stocks"* ya *"Achhe stocks batao"*
- **Market Trend**: 
    - *"How is the market?"* ya *"Market kaisa hai?"* (Nifty/BankNifty status)

---

## 🎮 UI & Button Controls (Click-less Control)
Ab aapko buttons par click karne ki zaroorat nahi hai:

- **Modal/Window Band Karna**: 
    - *"Close"*, *"Band karo"*, *"Exit"*, ya *"Hatao"*
- **Chart vs Analysis (Inside Modal)**:
    - *"Show Chart"* ya *"Graph dikhao"*
    - *"Show Analysis"* ya *"Insights dikhao"*
- **Watchlist Manager**:
    - *"Add to watchlist"* ya *"Watchlist mein dalo"* (Inside stock details)
- **Voice Alerts Toggle**:
    - *"Mute"* ya *"Voice band karo"*
    - *"Enable Voice"* ya *"Voice shuru karo"*
- **Layout Change**:
    - *"Table view"* ya *"Card view"*
    - *"Layout badlo"*

---

## 🔄 System Actions
- **Refresh Data**: 
    - *"Refresh dashboard"*, *"Data taza karo"*, ya *"Latest data lao"*
- **Run AI scan**: 
    - *"Run full analysis"* ya *"Scanning shuru karo"*

---

## 💡 Pro Tips:
1. **Mic Icon**: Pehle dashboard par bane **Mic button** par click karein, fir command bolein.
2. **Natural Voice**: Aap normal tareeke se bol sakte hain, system keywords ko NLP (AI) ke zariye pehchaan leta hai.
3. **Combination**: Aap bol sakte hain—*"Search Reliance"*... (wait for modal)... *"Show chart"*.
